About EcoPackAI

EcoPackAI is an AI-powered sustainable packaging recommendation system that helps businesses choose eco-friendly and cost-effective packaging materials based on specific product requirements. The system takes user inputs such as product type, product category, fragility level, shipping type, and sustainability priority to analyze the packaging needs of the product. Based on these inputs, it intelligently recommends the top five most suitable packaging materials using machine learning models. It also provides predicted packaging cost and estimated CO₂ emission scores for each recommended material. Additionally, the platform shows CO₂ reduction percentage along with material usage and cost comparison analytics. Users can further generate and export detailed sustainability reports in PDF or Excel format for documentation and decision-making purposes.
